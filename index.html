our story

Chapter 1: Almost haters

Meeting Anh in a school trip, my heart skipped a beat the moment I saw her. Striking up a conversation with her, I was jokey and talkative while she was quiet. The first impression was not really good.

Chapter 2: Make a move

With the Cupid’s arrow I was shoot, I had fallen for her. “I need a plan”. Yes. “I need a plan”. Getting to know about her, making friends with her friends, showing up at places she spent time at, I had a strategic plan BUT got “friend zoned”. That girl is a creature unlike any other. That girl! I had to make her mine.

Chapter 3: True love can thaw a frozen heart

Things were not really smooth in the first place, but a man in thirst for love would never give up on the girl of his dream. Chasing her, showering her with love, I did everything I could to earn her love. Time went by, from a friend, I was finally “levelled up” to be her boyfriend, on a summer day in 2016.

Chapter 5: Tie the knot

Nearly 4 years of ups and downs later, and we’ve already been back to Vietnam, starting our own career paths. A few months ago, I finally popped the questions while we were floating over Trang An River and Anh said yes. We can’t wait for this exciting new chapter to begin. No doubt there’ll be great times and of course difficult times ahead, but we do believe we can come through anything.
